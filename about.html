Our Story

Launched in 2025, Vastraa was born out of a simple yet powerful idea — to redefine men’s fashion by making premium style both affordable and accessible. What started as a humble venture in Vijayawada has evolved into a dynamic online store trusted by fashion-conscious customers across India. At Vastraa, we believe that looking sharp shouldn’t break the bank. Our dedicated team carefully curates high-quality apparel that blends comfort, trend, and value — ensuring you get the best without compromise. Driven by passion and powered by purpose, we remain committed to delivering an unmatched shopping experience. Every piece we offer, every decision we take, revolves around one goal: making you feel confident, stylish, and satisfied.

2025

Vastraa was founded with just 4 team members and 50 products and within a 2 months we have reached 50,000 customers and expanded nationwide

Our Mission

To provide high-quality fashion at affordable prices, making style accessible to everyone regardless of budget. We aim to simplify the shopping experience through innovative technology and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To become Andra's's leading online fashion destination, known for our quality products, ethical practices, and customer-first approach. We envision a future where shopping is effortless, enjoyable, and sustainable.
